# Procurement Analysis

# Problems
Procurement should be cost-efficient, compliant, and timely. 

There are key business challenges:
- Paying the right price for products
- Ensuring all invoices match purchase orders
- Managing vendor relationships to consolidate spend and maximize savings

# Objectives
1. Price Compliance
   - Measure price variance and total savings per vendor
   - Identify purchases below or above standard market prices
2. Process Compliance
   - Evaluate First0Time Match Rate(FTMR) for purchase order and invoice alignment
3. Strategic Spend Analysis
   -Classify cendors as strategic or tail based on total spend and transaction per vendor

# Insights
1. Total savings: Saving 3.96% of total spend implies a small saving
2. FTMR and Price Variance: accurate and transparent transaction
3. Spend-Saving Alignment: Higher transaction volume per vendor correlate with higher absolute savings.

# Recommendation
The company has an opportunity to further increase cost efficienty by strategically managing total spend. The high FTMR and valid price variance demonstrate a healthy procurement process, while the alignment between total spend and savings indicates strong business partnerships with key vendors. The company can leverage these partnership by consolidating purchases with strategic suppliers, negotiating better pricing, and strengthening vendor relationships to maximize both cost savings and operational efficiency.
